## Step 3: Create Your First Booking (5 minutes)

This is the core feature - let's get a booking in the system!

### Quick Book (Easiest Method)

1. Click **"Quick Book"** in top navigation
2. **Select a Boat**
   - Choose boat from dropdown
3. **Choose Date & Time**
   - Pick date (future dates only)
   - Pick time (e.g., 10:00 AM)
   - Pick duration (2h, 4h, 6h, 8h)
4. **Enter Customer Name**
   - First name
   - Last name
   - Email
   - Phone (optional)
5. **Select Package**
   - Charter only
   - With drinks (‚Ç¨X per person)
   - With food (‚Ç¨Y per person)
   - Premium (drinks + food)
6. **Set Passenger Count**
   - Number of people
7. **Assign Captain** (optional)
   - Select captain from dropdown
8. **Review Price**
   - Total shows all costs
9. **Confirm**
   - Booking created!
   - 15-minute hold starts
   - Email sent to customer

**‚úÖ Booking created! Next: Get payment**

## Step 4: Get Paid (3 minutes)

Your customer needs to pay. Two options:

### Option A: Send Payment Link (Fastest)

1. Go to **Bookings** page
2. Find your booking
3. Click on it to open detail
4. Click **"Send Payment Link"** button
5. Email sent to customer with Stripe payment link
6. Customer clicks link ‚Üí enters card ‚Üí pays
7. You get instant notification when paid ‚úì

### Option B: Record Manual Payment

1. Customer pays you (cash, transfer, etc.)
2. Go to **Bookings** detail page
3. Click **"Record Payment"**
4. Enter:
   - Payment method (Cash, Card, Transfer, etc.)
   - Amount paid
   - Transaction reference (optional)
5. Save
6. Booking marked as paid ‚úì

**Refund if Cancelled:**
- Customer cancels? System auto-calculates refund based on policy
- Click "Cancel Booking", system shows refund amount
- Confirm cancellation
- Refund processed automatically

## Step 5: Manage Your Fleet (3 minutes)

### Add a Boat

1. Click **"Fleet"** in navigation
2. Click **"+ Add New Boat"** button
3. Enter:
   - Boat name (required)
   - Type: Sailboat, Motorboat, or Jetski
   - Capacity: Max passengers
   - Optional: Description, photo
4. Click **"Create Boat"**

‚úÖ Boat added! Now customers can book it.

### Add Pricing for Boat

1. Click **"Pricing"** in navigation
2. Select your boat
3. For each duration (2h, 4h, 6h, 8h, full-day):
   - Enter price (e.g., ‚Ç¨200 for 2h)
4. Save pricing

‚úÖ Pricing set! Bookings will use these prices automatically.

   - **Sailor**: Crew member, gets hourly fee
   - **Sales Agent**: Creates bookings, gets commission
   - **Office Staff**: Administrative support
5. Set compensation:
   - **Hourly Rate**: For captains/sailors (‚Ç¨/hour)
   - **Commission**: For agents (% of booking price)
6. Click **"Create Agent"**
7. Email sent to new person with setup link

‚úÖ Team member added! They can now log in.

### Assign Captain to a Booking

1. When creating booking: Select captain from dropdown during booking creation
2. When editing booking: Click "Edit" ‚Üí Select captain ‚Üí Save

## Pro Tips ‚≠ê

1. **Use Quick Book for Speed**
   - Fastest way to create bookings
   - Best for simple charters
   - Advanced Book for complex crews

2. **Bulk Copy Pricing**
   - Set price on one boat
   - Copy to all boats at once
   - Saves time on pricing updates

3. **15-Minute Hold**
   - Bookings auto-reserve for 15 minutes
   - Prevents double-booking
   - Hold expires automatically
   - Customer has 15 min to pay

4. **Share Portal Link**
   - Customer can pay themselves
   - No payment processing by you
   - Customer can request changes
   - Secure token-based access

5. **Archive Instead of Delete**
   - Never actually delete staff/customers
   - Archive keeps historical data
   - Can reactivate later
   - Better for compliance

6. **Commission Tracking**
   - Set commission % per agent
   - Auto-calculated per booking
   - View in Reports
   - Track owed commission

7. **Calendar View**
   - See all boats on one calendar
   - Color-coded by status
   - Drag to reschedule
   - Month or week view

8. **Crew Assignments**
   - Multiple sailors per booking
   - One captain per booking
   - Cannot double-book crew
   - Crew sees in "My Bookings"

## Troubleshooting Quick Fixes

**Can't log in?**
- Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- Reset password via email

**Boat showing unavailable?**
- Check blocked slots (maintenance)
- Check captain isn't already booked
- Try different time

**Hold expired?**
- 15-min hold auto-expires
- Create new booking again
- Make sure to pay before hold expires

**Refund not showing?**
- Stripe refunds take 3-5 business days
- Check Stripe dashboard
- Or record manual refund

**Can't find crew member?**
- Check they're active (not archived)
- Check they have correct role
- Add them in Agents page first

**Payment not updating?**
- Wait 1-2 minutes for sync
- Refresh page
- Check Stripe dashboard
